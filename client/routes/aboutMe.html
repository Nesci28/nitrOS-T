My name is Marc Gagnon and i've been coding for almost 16 years in different languages. I even remember coding in Q-Basic on my father's 486 Turbo... I studied at College Rosemont in the "Analyst Programmer" course in 2003-2004. I don't know now, but at this time, the computers over there, were a yellow/brownish kind of dinosaurs and we were coding on Turbo Pascal. I worked on small and big projects but those were always personnal stuff or as a "freelance" for some startup companies.

I am an ex-military who struggled for a very long to find what I wanted to do once I got out of it but with nOS , I finally figured it out. I love to code and that's what, other then my kids, thrills me everyday. I wake up in the middle of the night because I dreamed about the solution I needed... I wake up in the morning with a new idea, thinking I would have enough time to code it while my coffee is brewing, to notice we are now in the middle of the night, 2 days later... Talking to my girlfriend about my latest projects and knowing, without a doubt, that she didn't get anything out of my gibberish... I am a passionnate.

One day, when I'll come back home, my house will detect me, unlock the door, turn on the lights, put the dinner in the over, call me Sir and, without forgetting, open up my latest WIP project. Wait... can't my rPi do that?... B.R.B.
